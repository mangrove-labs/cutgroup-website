Test websites & apps. Get paid.

The Miami Civic User Testing Group (CUTGroup Miami) is a community of Miami-Dade County residents
who get paid to test out civic websites and apps.

We need you. No technology experience needed.

  • Why should I test?

If an app doesn't work for you,
it doesn't work!

Why? Technology developers make websites, mobile apps, and other tools specifically for Miami-Dade County residents. As part of the CUTGroup, you'll help developers make their software better -- which will help make people's lives better.

Who? You! We are looking for residents from all over Miami-Dade County, who use different types of devices, browsers, and operating systems. No tech experience necessary!

Don't forget to tell your friends!

Where? Testing will happen all over Miami-Dade County in different locations. A network of more than 250 places in the city where internet and computer access, digital skills training, and online learning resources are available – for free.

Get started!

  1. Fill out the CUTGroup profile form below.
  2. Once you sign up, you will receive email or text notifications about new test opportunities. We will ask a few more questions that relate to the technology we are going to test, and you can let us know if you are available.
  3. We'll let you know when you're chosen to test civic websites and apps. Testing happens all over Miami-Dade County in public computer centers.
  4. Once you complete a test, you'll receive a $20 VISA gift card.

Sign up to test apps!

This survey asks about where you live, what devices you have, and how you connect to the Internet. This helps us get the right mix of people to test a particular application and website.

About

The Civic User Testing Group is a project of the Code for Miami, a civic organization devoted to using technology to make lives better in Miami. The project is supported by a 2016 Knight Cities Challenge grant provided by the Knight Foundation.

Want to be even more involved? If you're interested in getting involved in technology as a member of government, a technology developer, or an engaged citizen, consider joining monthly Code for Miami meetups. All are welcome!

Contact

Questions, comments, concerns? Hit us here: info@cutgroup.miami. Our Privacy Policy is also available.

If you need materials in accessible format, sign language interpreters, and/or any other accommodation to participate as a CUTGroup Miami tester or proctor, contact info@cutgroup.miami at least a week in advance so we can prepare accordingly. Thanks!
